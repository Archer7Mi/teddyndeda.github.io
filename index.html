About Me

I'm a L1 Cybersecurity Analyst, Ethical Hacker, and KCA University graduate with hands-on experience in threat intelligence, network security, and IT infrastructure. I completed an internship at the National Treasury of Kenya, where I worked across IT infrastructure management, networking, and computer information systems.

My approach is methodical: understand attacker techniques, simulate them in controlled lab environments, then engineer reliable detections. I build and document security labs across SOC operations, SIEM engineering, application security (OWASP Top 10), identity & access management, and GRC — so every project is a reproducible learning resource.
